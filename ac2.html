VERBS


What did we learn Today

Today we learnt that action verbs can be grouped into three buckets depending on whether and how many objects they take: Transitive verbs are those that take a direct object to make sense of itself. Intransitive verbs are those that do not take any object. Ditransitive verbs are those that take both a
direct and an indirect object. We studied direct and indirect objects. We learnt that direct objects answer the question “what?” and indirect objects answer the questions: “for whom?”, “to what?”, and “for what?”.
